### **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**

#### **General**

* **What exactly is CostCut AI?**
CostCut AI is an intelligent optimization layer for your AI workflows. It acts as a smart bridge between your queries and Cloud AI models, using local open-source models and advanced caching to filter, refine, and optimize prompts. This reduces the number of tokens sent to expensive cloud services, saving you money without compromising response quality.
* **Do I still get to use the AI models I love?**
Yes! CostCut AI doesn't replace your favorite Cloud models; it enhances them. You can continue using top-tier models through your own API keys, but with our optimization layer working in the background to ensure you only pay for what’s truly necessary.
* **Is it difficult to set up?**
Not at all. You can get started by simply selecting your preferred model from our integrated list (like Llama 3.2, Qwen 3, or Phi-3) and connecting your Cloud API keys. Our interface is designed to be intuitive for both developers and casual users.

#### **Technical & Savings**

* **How does "Saving Mode" actually save me money?**
Saving Mode uses a two-pronged approach:
1. **Local Pre-processing:** It uses lightweight local models to process simple tasks or "clean" complex prompts, preventing unnecessary cloud requests.
2. **Intelligent Caching:** The system learns from your frequent or similar queries. Instead of re-sending redundant prompts to the cloud, it retrieves optimized responses from its local cache, costing you zero cloud tokens.


* **Will my AI responses become less intelligent if I use CostCut AI?**
Absolutely not. CostCut AI is designed to preserve intelligence. For complex tasks, the system intelligently routes the core request to your high-performance Cloud model while using local models only for structuring, cleaning, or optimizing the context. You get the quality you expect with a smaller token bill.
* **Which models are supported?**
We currently support a robust suite of open-source models, including **Llama 3.2, Qwen 3, and Phi-3**. We are constantly adding support for new models as they become available.

#### **Budget & Control**

* **How do I control my monthly AI spending?**
You can set a **Monthly Token Limit** directly in the CostCut AI dashboard. Once you approach or reach this limit, the system can notify you or restrict further cloud calls, ensuring you never face an "end-of-month surprise" on your API bill.
* **Can I see exactly how much I am saving?**
Yes. Our built-in **Dashboard** provides real-time visualization of your token consumption. You can see a direct comparison of your costs *with* vs. *without* CostCut AI, showing you exactly how many tokens (and dollars) you’ve saved over time.

#### **Privacy & Security**

* **Is my data safe when using CostCut AI?**
Privacy is a core pillar of CostCut AI. Because we utilize local models, a significant portion of your data processing happens right on your machine, never leaving your environment. Your Cloud API keys are handled securely and are used strictly for the requests you authorize.
